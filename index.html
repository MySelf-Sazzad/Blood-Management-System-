About Ami Donor

The Problem

Every day, thousands of people lose their lives due to blood shortages. In emergency situations, finding the right blood type quickly can be the difference between life and death. Traditional blood banks often face shortages during critical times.

  • Shortage of rare blood types
  • Delayed response in emergencies
  • Lack of donor-recipient connection

Did You Know?

One blood donation can save up to three lives, and someone needs blood every two seconds.

Our Solution

Ami Donor bridges the gap between donors and recipients through real-time availability tracking and location-based matching.

How We Help

Ami Donor is a modern platform that connects voluntary blood donors with those in need. Our smart system tracks donation dates to ensure donor safety while maintaining an active database of available donors.

  • Real-time donor availability
  • Location-based search
  • Smart eligibility tracking

Join Our Mission

Whether you're a donor or seeking blood, Ami Donor is here to help. Together, we can ensure that no one suffers due to blood shortages.
